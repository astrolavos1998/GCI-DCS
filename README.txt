GCI - DCS v.5.06


- [ENG] -
The Lua script GCI - DCS version 5.06 simulates the operation of a GCI (Ground Controlled Interception) controller or an AWACS (Airborne Warning And Control System).
The main purpose of the program is to provide players (clients) with automatic and realistic reports of enemy air contacts (Bandits) and friendly air picture (Friendly Picture), based on data collected by friendly sensors (AWACS, ground radar, ships).

[!] WARNING: Use of GCI v.5.06.lua is at your own risk.
